# Soundless - Marketing Strategy (Threads)

## Target Audience

- Samsung Galaxy users in Korea
- People frustrated by the mandatory camera shutter sound
- Quiet photography enthusiasts (cafes, libraries, sleeping babies, pets)
- Content creators who need silent captures

## Key Selling Points

1. **No PC or USB required** — everything happens on-device
2. **One-tap toggle** — disable or re-enable in seconds
3. **Free to use** — ad-supported with optional $2.99 ad removal
4. **Persists after closing** — set once, forget until firmware update
5. **6 languages supported** — EN, KO, JA, AR, ZH-CN, ZH-TW

---

Post **1-2 times per day** for the first week, then **3-4 times per week** ongoing. Best times for Korean audience: **12:00-13:00 (lunch)** and **21:00-23:00 (evening)**.

## Engagement Tips

- **Reply to comments quickly** — early engagement boosts visibility on Threads
- **Search for related complaints** — find people complaining about shutter sound and reply helpfully (not spammy)
- **Cross-post to Korean communities** — consider posting on Naver Cafe (갤럭시 커뮤니티), DC Inside (갤럭시 갤러리), FM Korea
- **Create a short video** — screen recording of the setup process (5-step onboarding) works well for Threads video posts
- **Ask questions** — "셔터음 때문에 불편했던 적 있으시죠?" drives engagement better than just announcing
