Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Seongwoo Choi ("we", "our", or "us") built Soundless as a free, ad-supported app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Soundless itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All operations (ADB pairing, connection, and system setting changes) happen locally on your device. No data leaves your device through our app.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses data from apps.

Google Play Billing

We offer an in-app purchase to remove ads. This is processed through Google Play Billing. We do not have access to your payment information. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Advertising ID

Our app uses the Android Advertising ID through the Google Mobile Ads SDK for the purpose of serving ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings under Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization.

Data Storage

Soundless stores the following data locally on your device using SharedPreferences:

  • Onboarding completion status
  • Selected language preference
  • Ad removal purchase status

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Permissions

Soundless requires the following permissions:

  • INTERNET - Required for displaying ads and checking for app updates.
  • AD_ID - Used by the Google Mobile Ads SDK to serve ads.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
